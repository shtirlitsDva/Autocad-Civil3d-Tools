Roman chip — four palettes

The Series chip (I / II / III) shown in every label style, now in four high‑contrast schemes on true black — so the frames read clearly instead of melting into the background. Every panel is rendered live by the real Graphviz engine.

Segmented Tiles — two ways to make it readable

Right now the grout and the tiles are both near‑black, so the tiles barely separate. Option A inverts the roles — black grout, light tiles, dark ink (the most distinct treatment of the set). Option B keeps the dark tiles but outlines each in the palette’s frame color (legible, but closer to the other framed styles). Shown across all four palettes, Series II.
